Players involved in the professional League Scene

In this section, users can find information containing the history of various players that have competed to date. This information provides various statistics such as the username of the player, the players first and last name, what team they are playing for (if any), and what was their previous team (if any)

Notes for usage:

Users can directly add, edit, or delete entries using the provided buttons. Upon adding an entry, user must update values accordingly.

playerID is of type INT. currentTeam and previousTeam of are type INT and the corresponding value can be determined via the Teams page.
