Teams involved in the professional League Scene

In this section, users can find information containing the history of various teams that have competed to date. This information provides various statistics such as the name of the team, the year the team was established/disbanded, whether that team is currently playing, and what league they belong to.

Notes for usage:

Users can directly add, edit, or delete entries using the provided buttons. Upon adding an entry, user must update values accordingly.

teamID, yearEstablished, and yearEnded (can be null) is of type INT. leagueFK corresponds to the league that team belongs to
