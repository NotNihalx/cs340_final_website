Various Tournaments throughout the years

In this section, users can find various tournmanent data for various tournaments (both at an international/regional level), dating back to the earliest known tournmanet. Information included is as follows: Year the tournament took place, the location of the tournament, the name of the tournament, the format of the tournament, the teams involved, the winner, the reward/prize money, the number of participants, and the leagues involved.

Notes for usage:

Users can directly add, edit, or delete entries using the provided buttons. Upon adding an entry, user must update values accordingly.
